Fees

EXWAL takes a 1% fee for each successfully closed order. The fee will be calculated in BTC and will be taken from both sides: buyer and seller. The base for the fee calculation is the fact amount the order is closed of.

For example, you sell 1 BTC, regarding the fee calculation Policy, we will charge you at 1.01 BTC. If you are a buyer, in this case, you will get the 0.99 BTC. Exwal app controls the order amount and calculates the maximum quantity you can set.

How to generate a token

  1. Register the QIWI wallet for using it in EXWAL services
  2. Go to https://qiwi.com/api
  3. Sign in with your login credentials
  4. Click “Generate a new token” button
  5. Click “Choose all button”
  6. Do the confirmation on the next step (please be advised that Exwal stores your token encrypted and we do not provide any access to the customer’s data to any 3d Parties)
  7. Confirm the request by an SMS code sent to your phone number
  8. Add this token to the mobile app
  9. Start trading we will control the transfers
